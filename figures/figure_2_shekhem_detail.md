# Figure 2

## Caption
Detailed topographic and surface-drainage extraction for the immediate environs of Shekhem. Terrain features are derived from NASA SRTM 1 Arc-Second Global elevation datasets.

The figure identifies local terrain anomalies, modern city boundaries for spatial context, and key structural saddles. It does not map historical boundaries or definitive archaeological remains.

## Status
Methodological visualization.

## Important limitations
* Elevation routing is subject to standard 30-meter cell averaging.
* Minor localized erosion features or historic terrace systems may be obscured.
* Extracted drainage vectors represent mathematical flow potential rather than active historical watercourses.
* All regional point markers serve as tentative analytical annotations.
