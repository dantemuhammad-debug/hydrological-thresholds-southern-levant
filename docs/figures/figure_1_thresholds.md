# Figure 1

## Caption
Topographic and hydrological setting of Shekhem, the Yabboq corridor, and the Wadi Mūsā/Petra Basin. Terrain and drainage are derived from NASA SRTM 1 Arc-Second Global elevation data.

The figure identifies major drainage systems, watershed structure, terrain thresholds, and relevant locations. It does not propose a reconstructed historical itinerary.

## Status
Methodological visualization.

## Important limitations
* Drainage is extracted from surface elevation.
* Drainage lines do not establish perennial flow.
* Karst subsurface flow is not represented.
* The displayed watershed divide and basin boundary require further validation.
* Constriction markers are analytical annotations, not archaeological findings.
