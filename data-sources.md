## Data source

Terrain data used by this project are derived from NASA Shuttle Radar Topography Mission 1 Arc-Second Global products distributed by the U.S. Geological Survey Earth Resources Observation and Science Center.

The source SRTM data are identified by USGS as public domain. They are not included under the repository’s MIT software license.

USGS identifies the SRTM 1 Arc-Second products and related materials as public domain, and distributes the global data through EarthExplorer.
